To enter your case, enter a 9-digit number. The first digit is whether or not the centers are oriented (1 if they are, 0 if not). The next 2 digits represent the locations of the L and R edges, respectively. The locations are represented by the numbers 1-6, being the locations of the letters A, B, C, D, U, and W, in that order. The remaining 6 digits are the orientations of those same 6 pieces, in the same order (again, 1 for oriented, 0 for misoriented). Remember that for a case to be possible, there must be an even number of oriented edges (the last 6 digits must add to an even number).
